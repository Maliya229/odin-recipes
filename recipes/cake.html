Cake recipe

Description

A classic chocolate cake begins by whisking together flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, and a pinch of salt, then blending in eggs, milk, oil or butter, and a splash of vanilla to create a smooth, rich batter. Hot water or coffee is often added to deepen the chocolate flavor and give the cake a moist, tender texture. The batter is poured into greased pans and baked until soft and springy, filling the kitchen with a warm chocolate aroma. Once cooled, the cake is typically layered or coated with a creamy chocolate frosting or ganache, resulting in a decadent, indulgent dessert that’s perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup oil or melted butter
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot water or hot coffee

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease your cake pans.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil or melted butter, and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  4. Slowly add hot water or coffee and stir until the batter is smooth.
  5. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared pans.
  6. Bake for 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  7. Let the cake cool completely before frosting.
