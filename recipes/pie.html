Chocolate Pie Recipe

Description

A rich chocolate pie features a buttery, flaky crust filled with a smooth and creamy chocolate filling. The crust is first baked until golden, then filled with a luscious mixture of cocoa, sugar, milk, and eggs, cooked until thick and velvety. Once poured into the crust, the pie is chilled until set, creating a silky texture. It is often topped with whipped cream or chocolate shavings, making it a decadent dessert perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pie crust
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups milk
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream (optional, for topping)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven and bake the pie crust according to package instructions. Let it cool.
  2. In a saucepan, mix sugar, cocoa powder, cornstarch, and salt.
  3. Gradually whisk in milk and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly.
  4. In a separate bowl, lightly beat egg yolks, then slowly add a little hot mixture to temper them.
  5. Pour the egg mixture back into the saucepan and cook until thick.
  6. Remove from heat and stir in butter and vanilla extract.
  7. Pour the filling into the cooled crust and smooth the top.
  8. Refrigerate for at least 3–4 hours until set.
  9. Top with whipped cream before serving (optional).
